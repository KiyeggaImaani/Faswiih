FASWIIH MEDIA AND CHARITY ORGANISATION

The name of the organisation is FASWIIH MEDIA AND CHARITY ORGANISATION. It is a non profitable charity organisation which was formed on 16TH, JUNE, 2024. The location of the organisation is Namulesa, Jinja district in the Republic of Uganda.

The organisation has been created for an indefinate term in order to provide aid to the poor, support education, promote health care and construct mosques.

OUR FIRST DONATIONS

We made our first donations on 16th. August, 2024. We were able to visit Nansu village in Buikwe District were we gave out foods, sugar, soap and other requirement in order to improve the standards of the people of Nansu village .

We were also able to realise other challenges faced by the people of Namaganga village which included; water supply, shelter, and mosques .
